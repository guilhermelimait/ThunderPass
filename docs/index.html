What is it?

Your handheld, always connected.

Remember walking around with your Nintendo 3DS and suddenly getting that green light? ThunderPass does exactly that — for Android. Designed as a background service, it silently exchanges profiles with nearby players using Bluetooth Low Energy, no Wi-Fi or internet required.

Features

Everything you'd want from a modern StreetPass.

A growing feature set built around privacy, retro gaming, and pure fun — offline-first, always.

⚡

BLE Sparks

Automatic Bluetooth Low Energy discovery. When two ThunderPass users are near each other, they exchange profile cards silently — no action required.

✓ Live
🔒

Privacy by Design

Your real device ID is never broadcast. ThunderPass uses rotating IDs (HMAC-SHA256, 30-min windows) so you can't be passively tracked.

✓ Live
🔋

Joules Energy System

Every unique Spark earns you 100 Joules. Spend them in the Visual Shop to unlock profile effects, CRT Scanlines, Pixelated Auras, and more.

✓ Live
🏆

Badges & Achievements

Earn Thunder Badges for milestones across passes, retro gaming, geolocation, and integrations. Each badge tracks your progress with a glowing thunder bolt.

✓ Live
🕹️

RetroAchievements Sync

Link your RA account and automatically share your rank, points, and recently mastered games whenever you Spark with another player.

✓ Live
👻

Ghost Data

Share your current game and high score as a "Ghost". When two players have the same game in common, a Legendary Encounter is triggered.

✓ Live
🎨

Profile Maker

Craft your traveler identity — custom display name, avatar style, greeting message, and a randomly generated pixel art avatar if you want to keep it a surprise.

✓ Live
🔔

Encounter Notifications

Get an instant notification the moment a new ThunderPasser is identified nearby, even while the app is in the background.

Coming Soon
☁️

Cloud Sync & Web Profile

Optionally sign in to back up your profile and encounter history to the cloud. Share a link so anyone can look up your ThunderPass card online.

✓ Live
🗺️

Power Surge Events

Location-based 2× Joule multiplier events where the Grid goes electric. Show up, rack up energy, and unlock exclusive profile effects.

Planned
📡

Open Payload API

A future public SDK will let other apps piggyback on ThunderPass exchanges — imagine sharing your Pokémon team, board game stats, or high scores.

Planned
📱

Landscape-First UI

Every screen, every button, every card is designed for widescreen handheld play. The compact bottom bar stays fixed so you always know where you are.

✓ Live
Roadmap

Where we're headed.

A transparent look at what's shipped and what's next on the Grid.

M0–M1
Foundation & StreetPass MVP ✓ Shipped
Android project scaffold, BLE advertise + scan, rotating privacy IDs, GATT profile card exchange, Room database, brand theme, CI/CD pipeline, and unit tests.
M2
Energy, Trust & Quality ✓ Shipped
Joules energy engine, haptic "double-pulse Spark" feedback, home widget showing encounter counts, better onboarding copy, and improved battery handling.
M3
Fun Layer & Retro Gaming ✓ Shipped
Ghost payloads, RetroAchievements integration, dynamic profile cards with RA rank/points, special achievement triggers (Platinum Pulse, Legendary Encounter, Retro Circuit), and the Visual Shop.
M4
Accounts & Cloud Sync ✓ Shipped
Supabase email OTP sign-in, auto-sync profile after every Spark, web dashboard (this page!) for public profile lookup, post-encounter sync.
M5
UX Polish & Notifications In Progress
Landscape-first UI refinements, new ThunderPasser notification, profile avatar randomiser, encounter data cards moved to profile area, fixed landscape bottom bar, encounter backup/restore, remote privacy controls.
M6
Power Surge Events & Platform Planned
Location-based 2× energy multiplier events, public payload provider SDK so other apps can participate in ThunderPass exchanges, versioned protocol extensions.
